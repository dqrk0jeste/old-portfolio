About me

So right now I don't have any worries . I can focus on new things that interest me - and programming became one of them. As my high school profile is specialized in mathematics, physics and programming, I already had a really good foundation for it. That includes:


  • Two years of Java programming, including OOP and building GUIs using Java Swing.

  • A year-long course on computer architecture, history and assembly

I started with the basics: learned HTML, CSS and JavaScript, which I used for building this website. Then I learned Node.js for the backend and how to use databases, manually and with ORMs. On the frontend, I have learned Vue.js as my framework of choice to make my life easier.

Of course, there is so much more to learn. But I hope I will find a job soon, so I can say that all of this was worth it. And also to help my family financially! I am open to work preferably remotely, but hybrid work is also acceptable. I appreciate you reading this and hope to hear from you soon :)
